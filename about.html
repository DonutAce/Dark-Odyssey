ABOUT THE GAME

The world broke when the stars vanished and the Luminara shattered.

Dark Odyssey is a dark fantasy journey about a marked wanderer who wakes in ruins, faces the Hollow, and is pulled into the war between light, darkness, and the ancient will of the Veil King.

CORE HOOK

The sky had no stars the night the world broke.

No one remembered when it truly began. The light behind the world, the Luminara, shattered, the darkness awakened, and you rose from a ruined village with no memory of your name, your past, or why a mark on your hand still glowed in the ash.

  • Audience Made for players who want haunting lore, active combat, and a story driven by mystery, ruin, and choice.
  • Genre Dark fantasy, RPG progression, visual novel storytelling, and pressure-driven combat shape the experience.
  • Antagonist The Veil King waits behind the broken seal, while corrupted survivors and Hollow creatures spread the fall of the world.
MAIN STORY

You awaken with a mark that answers the dark.

When the first Hollow finds you, light erupts from your hand and tears it apart, revealing that you are one of the few still marked by the force that once held the world together.

CORE FEEL

Story-first, but built on tension and consequence.

Every encounter pushes you deeper into a ruined world where survivors fear each other, darkness offers power, and each step pulls you closer to the truth behind the cycle.

LORE TIMELINE

The fall of the world unfolds in six dark turning points.

This timeline gives the story a clearer shape, letting players and viewers follow the rise of the Hollow, the truth of the Luminara, and the final choice waiting at the end of the journey.

01

The Luminara Shatters

The deeper light that once held the world together breaks, and the darkness does not merely spread. It awakens.

02

The Marked One Awakens

You rise in the ruins with no memory of your past, carrying only a glowing mark that still answers the broken light.

03

The Hollow Begin To Rise

Twisted creatures and corrupted survivors spread across the land as fear, power, and the Gift reshape what remains of humanity.

04

The Truth Buried In Ruins

In the fallen capital, the truth emerges: the Luminara was never just a source of light. It was a seal.

05

The Veil King Awakens

Beyond the cracking sky, the ancient presence behind the cycle reveals itself and calls the world back into ruin.

06

The Final Choice Beyond Both

The ending is not about choosing light or darkness, but breaking the cycle itself and becoming something the world has never seen before.

FEATURE SIGNAL

The full experience is driven by mystery, corruption, and a choice beyond light or darkness.

These highlights show how the lore and gameplay work together for players who want atmosphere, challenge, and a strong narrative core.

NARRATIVE

The fall of the world gives every battle real stakes.

The conflict is not just combat for its own sake. The Luminara has shattered, the world is collapsing, and every encounter carries the weight of what was lost.

EVOLUTION

Corruption spreads through monsters and survivors alike.

The Hollow are only the beginning. Some humans embrace the Gift, gain terrifying strength, and slowly lose their memories, emotions, and humanity.

CHALLENGE

Victory depends on will, not strength alone.

The final struggle is not simply about defeating darkness, but about breaking an endless cycle and becoming something beyond both sides.
