TUTORIAL

Learn the controls, the combat rhythm, and the progression loop.

Dark Odyssey is built around quick movement, basic attacks, and two skills with cooldowns. Your stats decide how strong you are, and enemies scale with your growth.

MOVEMENT

Walk and jump to control space.

Use A/D to move and Space to jump. Staying grounded keeps you safe from boss patterns.

ATTACK

Basic attack is your steady damage.

Click to basic attack. Use it to finish mobs while you wait for your skills to come back.

SKILLS

Q and E are powerful but on cooldown.

Press Q for Shadow Strike and E for Void Cyclone. Watch cooldowns, spend mana, and punish openings.

PROGRESSION

Stats matter. Upgrades change the fight.

After wins, you gain points that can be spent on Health, Mana, Void Cyclone, and Shadow Strike. Stronger stats also mean tougher enemies.

STEP 01

Enter the lobby.

Talk to NPCs, manage upgrades, then approach the portal when you are ready.

STEP 02

Clear the battlefield.

Use clicks for consistent damage and save Q/E for when the boss is vulnerable.

STEP 03

Return stronger.

After a clear, you come back to the lobby, level up, and spend points to prepare for what comes next.
